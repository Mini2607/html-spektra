üçõ Butter Chicken

Category: Dinner

Ingredients:

  • 500g boneless chicken
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • 1 tbsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, salt, and garam masala. Let it rest for 2 hours.
  2. Cook marinated chicken in butter until golden brown.
  3. Add tomato puree and cook until it thickens.
  4. Stir in fresh cream and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with naan or rice.

ü•î Aloo Paratha

Category: Breakfast

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups wheat flour
  • 3 boiled potatoes
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tbsp chopped coriander
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Butter for cooking

Instructions:

  1. Knead wheat flour with water to make a soft dough.
  2. Mix mashed potatoes with spices and coriander.
  3. Roll out dough, stuff with potato mixture, and seal.
  4. Cook on a hot pan with butter until golden brown.
  5. Serve with yogurt or pickle.

üçõ Paneer Tikka

Category: Appetizer

Ingredients:

  • 250g paneer (cottage cheese)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil

Instructions:

  1. Cut paneer into cubes and mix with yogurt, spices, and lemon juice.
  2. Marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Skewer paneer pieces and grill them until golden brown.
  4. Serve hot with mint chutney and onion slices.
